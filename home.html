Vivanta Bhubaneswar, DN Square

Located in Bhubaneshwar, 20.9 km from Janardana Temple, Vivanta Bhubaneswar DN Square provides accommodations with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness center and a bar. With free WiFi, this 5-star hotel offers a concierge service and a tour desk. The property has a 24-hour front desk, room service and currency exchange for guests. Guest rooms in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV and a hairdryer. A buffet breakfast is available daily at Vivanta Bhubaneswar DN Square. The nearest airport is Biju Patnaik International Airport, 6 km from the accommodation. Vivanta Bhubaneswar DN Square has been welcoming Booking.com guests since Jul 21, 2021 Hotel chain/brand: The Indian Hotels Co Ltd

Mayfair Lagoon

MAYFAIR Lagoon is a marvellous property and one of the best luxury hotels in Bhubaneswar. This top hotel in Bhubaneswar offers lavish luxury as well as wonderful service. MAYFAIR Lagoon is a 5 Star Deluxe Hotel that is spread out across 10 acres in the heart of the capital city of Odisha. There are various categories of luxury accommodation available to suit everyone’s needs from the opulent lagoon side villas to family friendly suites and cottages to comfortable well-appointed club rooms. There are state-of-the-art conference rooms, banquet halls and lush outdoor venues to host meetings, seminars and weddings.

Trident Bhubaneswar

Trident, Bhubaneswar, is an ideal base to explore the sandy beaches, the 5 star attractions or the ancient temples of Odisha. Trident, Bhubaneswar is located approximately five kilometers from the central Bhubaneswar, 20 minutes from Biju Paitnaik international airport and close to many of the city’s top attractions. This five star hotel is an oasis of luxury, set amidst landscaped gardens and fruit orchards.

Swosti Premium

Swosti Premium is a luxurious business class hotel in Bhubaneswar that has been bestowed with 5 stars. It features 147 well-appointed rooms with modern hotel facilities and amenities, 8 banquet halls and 1 outdoor venue, 2 immaculate multicuisine restaurants and top-of-the-class facilities like a swimming pool, a Scottish themed bar, a discotheque, a health club and a beauty salon and spa. As an oasis of unparalleled luxury combined with excellent service, you can surely find a tranquil corner within the premises to enjoy your holiday retreat to its maximum. Here at Swosti Premium, the guests can find their way through the vast, intense and diversified culture of one of the oldest cities in India.

Welcomhotel by ITC Hotels, Bhubaneswar

Drawing inspiration from the city’s magnificent temples that number over 500, Welcomhotel Bhubaneswar is a visual ode to the architectural traditions and eventful history of the Golden Triangle of the East. Located just eight kilometres from the airport, the hotel offers sprawling lawns and exquisite accommodation. For the gourmet, the hotel offer local delicacies along with Indian & international cuisines at its award-winning restaurants. State-of-the-art banqueting facilities add another dimension to its appeal.
